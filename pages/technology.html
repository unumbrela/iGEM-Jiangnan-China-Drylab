CytoFlow Framework Overview

Our CytoFlow framework integrates three AI models forming a complete pipeline from fermentation optimization to sequence evolution

1

CytoGrow Model

At the fermentation level, in collaboration with wet lab, CytoGrow uses mathematical modeling to simulate cell growth and glucose consumption, achieving over 25% improvement in yeast biomass and providing crucial data support for wet lab experiments.

  • Mathematical modeling of cell growth
  • Glucose consumption simulation
  • 25%+ biomass improvement
  • Wet-dry lab collaboration support
2

CytoGuard Model

At the molecular level, CytoGuard utilizes Attention Mechanism to integrate 3 protein language models with HyperGraph technology to predict antimicrobial peptide activity (MIC), ensuring accurate assessment of peptide effectiveness.

  • 3 Protein Language Models integration
  • Attention Mechanism fusion
  • HyperGraph for structure analysis
  • MIC (activity) prediction
3

CytoEvolve Model

Finally, CytoEvolve combines Diffusion Model and Reinforcement Learning to generate improved LL-37 variants. After wet lab validation, the variants achieved comprehensive enhancements in antimicrobial activity, stability, and reduced cytotoxicity.

  • Diffusion Model for generation
  • Reinforcement Learning optimization
  • Improved LL-37 variants
  • Wet lab validated results
