Target Medical Institutions

Focused on Developing Regions

China reports approximately 475,000 new leukemia cases annually, with mid- to low-tier hospitals and developing regions lacking sufficient resources for traditional detection methods. CytoFlow's mission is to provide accessible, efficient, and accurate detection solutions for these areas, ensuring every patient can access high-quality medical services.

Through technology innovation and cost optimization, we're breaking down healthcare resource barriers and promoting the popularization of medical technology, making precision diagnosis no longer an exclusive right of large medical institutions.
